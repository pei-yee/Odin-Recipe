Hotpot Noodles

Ingredients

  • Sesame Paste
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chinese Vinegar
  • Chili Oil
  • Raw Garlic
  • Chopped Scallions
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • A handful of Leafy Greens
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 serving of noodles

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing any combination of sesame paste, soy sauce, sacha sauce, chinese vinegar, chili oil, garlic, scallions, cilantro according to preference and add any other ingredients of choice.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add oil to the pot, and blanch the leafy greens for 30 to 60 seconds, depending on the tenderness of leafy greens used.
  3. Remove the veggies from the water and transfer to your bowl.
  4. Boil the noodles according to package instructions, then add to the bowl. Toss everything together, and enjoy!
